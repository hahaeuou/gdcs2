# 1: Interface

While on the website or localhost, navigate to https://www.gdcreatorschool.com/admin. You should be prompted with a page asking you to log into Github

(img)

You will be prompted to log into Github, and authorize the Netlify CMS. Do both of these steps (if you don't have a Github account, you should create one now)

(img)

Once you log in, you should see a page like this. Each page under "Guides" corresponds to a unit, such as Triggers 1.

(img)

Clicking on a unit will bring up all guides in that unit.

(img)

Clicking on a guide brings up the editing menu, which lets you make changes to guides.

(img)

Be sure to save your changes frequently by clicking the "Save" button.

(img)

To make a new guide, click the "New Guides" button at the top. This will give you a fresh page.

(2 imgs)

Most of these fields are self-explanatory, but some do require more explanation.
- Draft: Whether the guide should be committed as a draft. This saves your work to the repository, but does not publish it on the website.
- Weight: The position your guide will have in its section. High-weight items display underneath items with lower weight.
- Math: Whether to enable math formatting in this guide. (Optional)

# 2: Workflow

Once your changes are made, they'll automatically be pushed to the Github repository. We'll review your changes and choose whether to accept your changes.
